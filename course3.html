AI Mastery Course

This comprehensive 90-day program is designed for those who want to master advanced AI concepts. It covers deep learning, NLP, computer vision, and real-world projects to prepare you for a professional career in AI.

Course Details

The AI Mastery Course is SuffixAI's flagship program for aspiring AI professionals. Over 90 days, you will dive deep into the algorithms and frameworks that power modern AI. The curriculum is structured to provide a strong theoretical foundation combined with intensive, hands-on projects.

This course is ideal for students and professionals with some prior knowledge of AI or programming who wish to become experts. You will work on real-world projects, guided by Prof. Gajanan Sawdtakar, to build a portfolio that stands out to employers.

Syllabus

  • Module 1: Advanced Machine Learning

    Weeks 1-3

    Revisiting ML fundamentals, advanced regression and classification, model optimization techniques, and ensemble methods like Gradient Boosting and Random Forest.

  • Module 2: Deep Learning Fundamentals

    Weeks 4-6

    Introduction to neural networks, backpropagation, and popular frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch. Building and training a basic neural network from scratch.

  • Module 3: Computer Vision (CV)

    Weeks 7-9

    Image processing, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), object detection (YOLO, R-CNN), and image segmentation. Includes a hands-on project on building an image classifier.

  • Module 4: Natural Language Processing (NLP)

    Weeks 10-12

    Text processing, sentiment analysis, sequence models, and an in-depth look at Transformer models (BERT, GPT). Includes a project on building a text summarizer.

  • Module 5: Capstone Project & Deployment

    Final Weeks

    A major, real-world project of your choice (e.g., building a recommendation engine, a chatbot, or an object detection system) and learning how to deploy your model into a production environment.
