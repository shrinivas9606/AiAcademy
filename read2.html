Blockchain Technology - Web 3

The Blockchain Technology – Web 3 Course by Suffix Academy is a 45-day program led by Prof. Nishant Chinchole. It focuses on blockchain fundamentals, cryptocurrency, smart contracts, and decentralized applications. The course is designed for students and professionals who want to build expertise in the fast-growing Web 3.0 ecosystem.

Course Details

The Blockchain Technology - Web 3 Course is designed to demystify the concepts behind this revolutionary technology. Led by industry expert Prof. Nishant Chinchole, the course provides a balanced approach of theoretical knowledge and practical application.

You will explore key concepts such as cryptographic principles, distributed ledger technology, smart contracts, and decentralized finance (DeFi). The program is project-based, giving you the skills needed to build and deploy your own blockchain applications.

Syllabus

  • Module 1: Blockchain Fundamentals

    Weeks 1-2

    Introduction to Blockchain, Cryptography and Hashing, Types of Blockchains, and Consensus Mechanisms (e.g., Proof of Work, Proof of Stake).

  • Module 2: Cryptocurrency and Wallets

    Weeks 2-3

    Understanding Bitcoin and Ethereum, managing digital wallets, and exploring tokenomics and different types of tokens (fungible vs. non-fungible).

  • Module 3: Smart Contracts and Solidity

    Weeks 3-4

    Introduction to smart contracts, learning the Solidity programming language, and deploying a simple smart contract on a test network.

  • Module 4: Web 3.0 and DApps

    Weeks 4-5

    What is Web 3.0? Exploring decentralized applications (DApps), building a basic DApp, and understanding the role of IPFS and decentralized storage.

  • Module 5: Project & Final Certification

    Final Week

    A capstone project where you will apply all learned concepts to build a real-world Web 3.0 application, followed by course certification.
